About

After working as a filmaker for over a decade, I learned to code. Oddly enough, software tickles my engineering-analytical side, as well as my creative side.

Projects that most interest me revolve around video or have interactive elements.

I live in Bozeman, Montana.

Currently

Currently, I am a core backend developer of PixelStrings, a platform by Cinnafilm. PixelStrings specializes cloud video processing and delivery. My job is two fold - automating the allocation and orcehstration cloud compute resources for processing and delivery, and creating APIs to enable different type of video conversion formats. These services are accesed by the front end UI, as well as our public API.
